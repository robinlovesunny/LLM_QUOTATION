# Product Overview

## 报价侠系统 (LLM Quotation System)

An intelligent quotation platform designed for Alibaba Cloud BTE/SA personnel that leverages AI capabilities and automation to compress traditional 7-day quotation work into a simple "Select, Configure, Export" three-step process.

## Core Features

- **AI-Powered Interaction**: Natural language understanding using Alibaba Cloud Bailian API (Qwen-Max) for automatic entity extraction from customer requirements
- **Intelligent Product Recommendation**: Smart product configuration recommendations with support for LLM token-based billing
- **Automated Price Calculation**: Complex billing rules including thinking/non-thinking modes, batch call discounts, and tiered pricing
- **Competitor Price Crawling**: Automated crawling of product pricing data from Alibaba Cloud, Volcano Engine, and other platforms
- **Quote Export**: One-click Excel quote generation with Alibaba Cloud OSS upload support

## Target Users

- Alibaba Cloud Business Technology Engineers (BTE)
- Solution Architects (SA)
- Sales teams requiring automated quotation generation

## Business Value

- Reduces quotation preparation time from 7 days to minutes
- Standardizes pricing calculations across complex billing scenarios
- Provides competitive intelligence through automated price monitoring
- Ensures consistency in quote formatting and delivery
